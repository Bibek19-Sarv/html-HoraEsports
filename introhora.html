About Horaa Esports

As a rising force in the Nepalese esports scene, Horaa Esports aims to compete at both national and international levels, striving to make a mark in the competitive gaming ecosystem. The organization is known for assembling skilled players, implementing strategic gameplay, and fostering a strong gaming community.Despite being a relatively new team, Horaa Esports has shown promising performances in local and regional tournaments. Beyond competition, Horaa Esports seeks to promote gaming as a viable career path in Nepal, fostering an engaged fanbase and community.
